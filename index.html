Tribute to Cats

Cats are fascinating creatures with many interesting traits and behaviors.
Did you know that.....

  • Cats have a specialized collarbone that allows them to always land on their feet when they fall.
  • A group of cats is called a clowder.
  • Cats sleep for an average of 12-16 hours a day.
  • Cats have five toes on their front paws but only four toes on their back paws.
  • The world's oldest cat on record lived to be 38 years and 3 days old.
  • A cat's nose is as unique as a human's fingerprint, with a distinct pattern of bumps and ridges.
  • Cats have a specialized reflective layer behind their retinas called the tapetum lucidum, which enhances their night vision.
  • The average cat can jump up to six times its body length in a single leap.
  • Cats have a specialized grooming tool on their tongue called papillae, which helps clean their fur and remove loose hair.
  • Cats have a strong sense of hearing and can detect frequencies ranging from 48 Hz to 85 kHz.
